The Weather page provides a list of recent reports received.

There are two lists available for reference:

  • Watch List contains all weather received for locations included in your watch list
  • Recent Reports contains the most recent 10 reports received of any type for any location

The Watch List setting is found on the Settings page.

NOTE: When this page becomes active (aka it is selected from the menu) it will display only new weather. Older weather and existing weather will not appear until their next report.
